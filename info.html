about konvrt

konvrt is a multi-purpose unit conversion site. The underlying goal of the site is to provide a simple to use and sleek interface, while still maintaining maximum practicality to the user in the act of unit conversion. It aims to have formulas to convert as many units as possible, from meters and pounds, to chains and parsecs. The simplicity of the design really is the key feature here.
